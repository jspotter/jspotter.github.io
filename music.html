Mazurka for Three Guitars (2014)

This piece was the result of a weekly challenge my friends and I established while traveling in Southeast Asia. The challenge was to create something that related to three randomly generated nouns. Here are the three nouns that inspired this piece, along with a description of why each one is relevant:

Polish - The piece is a mazurka, a folk dance of Polish origin.
Draw - The piece can be thought of as a battle between major and minor which ends in a draw.
Disease - I tried to create the musical equivalent of a healthy organism that gradually becomes infiltrated by harmful foreign agents.
